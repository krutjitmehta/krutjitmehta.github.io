What We'll Cover

This crash course is built around the Free Response Question (FRQ) section of the AP Calculus AB exam — the part most students find hardest to prepare for on their own. We'll work through several example problems that appear every year, break down exactly what the AP graders are looking for, and build a clear strategy for earning partial credit even when you're not sure of the full solution.

The session is designed for students who have already covered the core material in class and want a focused, high-efficiency review in the days before the exam. No fluff — just the concepts and techniques that show up most reliably on test day.

Topics at a Glance

FRQ structure and scoring breakdown
Derivatives and their applications
Definite and indefinite integrals
Area between curves
Accumulation and rate problems
Partial credit strategies
